VTOL Drone Design Project

Complete mechanical design of a VTOL (Vertical Take-Off and Landing) drone, with a strong focus on aerodynamics, CAD modeling and system integration.

Overview

This project involved the complete mechanical design of a VTOL drone, from early concept to detailed CAD models. Emphasis was placed on aerodynamic efficiency, modularity and system-level integration.

The project was carried out using professional engineering tools and simulation environments.

Key Highlights

  • Full mechanical design using CREO
  • Nacelle and fuselage CAD development
  • Aerodynamic optimization workflow
  • Simulation with MATLAB & MotionView
  • Mechanical system integration

Nacelle Design Focus

The nacelle was designed to balance aerodynamic performance, structural robustness and ease of integration. Several design iterations were evaluated through simulations and CAD refinements.
